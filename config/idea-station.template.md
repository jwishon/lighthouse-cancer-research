# Idea station

*Open questions and topics you want Lighthouse to chase over time. Add anything — a treatment you read about, a question for the next appointment, a "keep an eye on this" trial. The worker reads this alongside the profile and works your list into its daily search.*

## How to use this

- One idea per line or short block.
- Be specific where you can ("any new data on [drug] for [cancer type]") but rough is fine too.
- Mark things done or stale and move them to the bottom when they're no longer useful.

## Open questions

- *(example) Any newly recruiting trials within driving distance for this diagnosis and stage?*
- *(example) New evidence on [specific drug or combination] since last month?*
- *(example) What does the latest guidance say about [side effect / supportive care question]?*

## Watching

- *(example) Results expected from [trial name or ID] — flag when they publish.*
